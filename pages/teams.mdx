## Getting Involved

Interested in joining one of our ministry teams? 

1. Speak with the relevant team leader after service
2. Attend a training session
3. Start by shadowing experienced team members
4. Gradually take on more responsibilities as you learn

Each team has specific training requirements and ongoing development opportunities to help you serve with excellence.
