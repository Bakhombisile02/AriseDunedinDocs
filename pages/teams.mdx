# Ministry Teams

Welcome to the ministry teams section. Each team plays a vital role in creating an excellent worship experience for our congregation and online audience.

- **Spiritual Gifts:** Administration, Serving, Technical Skills

- **Skills Needed:** Basic technical aptitude, attention to detail

### Ministry Teams

#### Welcome Team
Creates a warm, welcoming environment for all who enter our church.
- **Spiritual Gifts:** Hospitality, Encouragement, Evangelism
- **Time Commitment:** Arrive early Sunday mornings
- **Skills Needed:** Friendly demeanor, heart for people

#### Kids Ministry
Provides engaging, age-appropriate programs for children during services.
- **Spiritual Gifts:** Teaching, Pastoring, Serving, Faith
- **Time Commitment:** Sunday mornings + occasional events
- **Skills Needed:** Heart for children, patience, creativity

#### Youth Ministry
Mentors and disciples teenagers through programs and relationships.
- **Spiritual Gifts:** Teaching, Pastoring, Encouragement, Faith
- **Time Commitment:** Weekly youth programs + events
- **Skills Needed:** Heart for teens, mentoring ability

#### Worship Team
Leads congregational worship through music and creative arts.
- **Spiritual Gifts:** Creative Arts, Leadership, Encouragement
- **Time Commitment:** Weekly practice + Sunday services
- **Skills Needed:** Musical ability, heart for worship

#### Prayer Team
Provides intercessory prayer support for church and community needs.
- **Spiritual Gifts:** Faith, Intercession, Discernment
- **Time Commitment:** Regular prayer meetings + as needed
- **Skills Needed:** Heart for prayer, confidentiality

#### Pastoral Care Team
Offers support, encouragement, and practical help to church members.
- **Spiritual Gifts:** Pastoring, Mercy, Encouragement, Helps
- **Time Commitment:** Flexible based on needs
- **Skills Needed:** Compassion, good listening skills

## Getting Involved

Ready to find your place on a team? 

**[🔍 Find Your Team →](/teams/join)** - Use our interactive tool to discover teams that match your spiritual gifts and interests.

### General Steps to Join:

1. **Discover** - Explore our teams and find one that matches your gifts and passions
2. **Connect** - Speak with the relevant team leader after service
3. **Learn** - Attend a training session or orientation
4. **Serve** - Start by shadowing experienced team members
5. **Grow** - Gradually take on more responsibilities as you develop

Each team has specific training requirements and ongoing development opportunities to help you serve with excellence.
