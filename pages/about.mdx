# About ARISE Church Dunedin

ARISE Church Dunedin is part of the wider ARISE Church family, committed to seeing lives transformed through the power of Jesus Christ.

## Our Story

We are a community of believers passionate about worship, fellowship, and serving our local community in Dunedin. Our church operates with the support of dedicated volunteers across multiple ministry teams.

## Documentation Purpose

This documentation hub serves our volunteer teams by providing:

- **Clear procedures** for all technical operations
- **Troubleshooting guides** for common issues
- **Training materials** for new team members
- **Best practices** developed through experience

## Teams We Support

Our documentation currently covers:

- **AV Team** - Audio-visual operations and streaming
- **Sound Team** - Audio mixing and system management
- **Packdown Team** - Event setup and breakdown

More team documentation will be added as we continue to grow and develop our resources.
