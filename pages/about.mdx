# About ARISE Church Dunedin

ARISE Church Dunedin is part of the wider ARISE Church family, committed to seeing lives transformed through the power of Jesus Christ. We are a vibrant community of believers passionate about worship, fellowship, and serving our local community in Dunedin.

## Our Story

ARISE Church exists to see lives transformed through the power of Jesus Christ. We believe that every person has value and purpose, and we are committed to creating an environment where people can encounter God, grow in their faith, and discover their unique calling.

Our Dunedin campus operates with the incredible support of dedicated volunteers across multiple ministry teams, each playing a vital role in creating an atmosphere where people can experience God's love and grace.

## Our Mission & Vision

**Mission:** To see lives transformed through the power of Jesus Christ.

**Vision:** Building a community where everyone can find their place and purpose in God's kingdom.

## Our Values

At ARISE Church Dunedin, our values guide everything we do:

### Excellence
We strive for excellence in everything we do, believing that our God deserves our very best. This doesn't mean perfection, but rather a commitment to continuous improvement and wholehearted dedication.

### Community
We believe in the power of authentic relationships. We are better together, supporting one another through life's journey and celebrating God's goodness as a family.

### Growth
We are committed to growing in our faith, our abilities, and our impact. We encourage personal development and spiritual maturity in every area of life.

### Service
We serve with joy and dedication, believing that serving others is a reflection of Christ's love. Every volunteer role, no matter how small, contributes to our collective mission.

## Volunteer Code of Conduct

As a volunteer at ARISE Church Dunedin, we ask that you commit to:

### Character & Integrity
- Demonstrate Christ-like character in all interactions
- Maintain honesty and integrity in all dealings
- Respect confidentiality when appropriate
- Handle conflicts with grace and maturity

### Excellence in Service
- Arrive on time and prepared for your volunteer role
- Communicate effectively with team leaders and members
- Take ownership of your responsibilities
- Continuously seek to improve your skills and knowledge

### Safe Environment
- Prioritize the safety and wellbeing of all participants
- Follow all safety protocols and procedures
- Report any concerns immediately to leadership
- Maintain appropriate boundaries in all relationships

### Team Unity
- Support and encourage fellow volunteers
- Work collaboratively towards common goals
- Embrace feedback and coaching
- Celebrate the successes of others

## Volunteer Expectations & Requirements

### Training Requirements
All volunteers are expected to:
1. **Complete orientation training** - Understanding our church vision, values, and expectations
2. **Attend team-specific training** - Learning the skills needed for your ministry area
3. **Participate in ongoing development** - Regular training updates and skill enhancement
4. **Shadow experienced volunteers** - Learning through observation and mentorship

### Commitment Expectations
- **Regular attendance** - Consistency in your volunteer schedule
- **Advance notice** - Communicate any schedule changes or conflicts
- **Annual commitment** - We ask for a minimum one-year commitment to allow for proper training and team development
- **Feedback participation** - Engage in regular check-ins and evaluation processes

### Safe Church Requirements
ARISE Church is committed to providing a safe environment for everyone. All volunteers must:
- Complete a police background check (for certain roles)
- Attend Safe Church training
- Understand and follow our child protection policies
- Report any concerns through proper channels

## Health & Safety

The safety of our volunteers and congregation is paramount:

### General Safety
- All equipment must be inspected before use
- Proper lifting techniques must be used for heavy items
- Emergency exits and procedures must be known
- First aid kits and emergency contacts are readily available

### Equipment Safety
- Only trained personnel should operate technical equipment
- Safety equipment (ear protection, safety glasses) must be used when required
- Regular maintenance schedules must be followed
- Any damaged equipment must be reported immediately

### Incident Reporting
- All incidents must be reported to team leaders immediately
- Accident forms must be completed for any injuries
- Near-miss events should be documented for prevention
- Regular safety reviews help us improve our procedures
