## Not Sure About Your Spiritual Gifts?

If you're unsure about your spiritual gifts, that's perfectly normal! Here are some ways to discover them:

### Self-Assessment Questions
- What activities energize you rather than drain you?
- What do others often compliment you on or ask for your help with?
- What needs in the church community stir your heart?
- When have you felt most fulfilled while serving?

### Resources for Discovery
- **Talk with a Pastor** - Our pastoral team can help you identify your gifts
- **Try Different Teams** - Many people discover their gifts through serving
- **Online Assessments** - There are many free spiritual gifts assessments available
- **Prayer and Reflection** - Ask God to reveal how He's gifted you

### Getting Started
Even if you're still discovering your gifts, don't wait to get involved! Many people find their calling through:
- **Helping where needed** - Start with practical serving opportunities
- **Supporting existing team members** - Learn by watching and assisting
- **Attending team meetings** - Get a feel for different ministries
- **Prayer and encouragement** - Support teams through prayer and presence

## Next Steps

1. **Pray** - Ask God to guide you to the right ministry opportunity
2. **Connect** - Reach out to team leaders that interest you
3. **Visit** - Attend a team meeting or training session
4. **Start Small** - Begin with a low-commitment role to test the fit
5. **Grow** - Develop your skills and take on greater responsibilities over time

Remember, serving is a journey of discovery. Your ministry involvement may evolve as you grow in faith and experience.
