## Purpose

Our AV system delivers video and audio to multiple destinations:
- Main hall projection
- Parents' room feed  
- Online streaming to other campuses

## Team Responsibilities

The AV team is responsible for:

- **Video Production** - Operating cameras and video switching
- **Graphics Management** - Managing song lyrics and sermon slides  
- **Live Streaming** - Connecting to other campuses for shared services
- **Equipment Setup** - Ensuring all systems are properly connected
- **Quality Control** - Monitoring audio and video quality throughout services

## Safety First

Before operating any equipment:
- Ensure all connections are secure
- Handle equipment with care
- Report any damaged cables or equipment immediately
- Never force connections or controls

## Training Requirements

All AV team members should:
1. Complete this documentation training
2. Shadow experienced operators for at least 3 services
3. Demonstrate competency in setup procedures
4. Understand troubleshooting basics
