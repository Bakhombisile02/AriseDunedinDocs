# System Overview

This section explains the purpose of each key component and how they work together to deliver video and audio to the main hall, the parents' room, and online streams.

## Core Components and Signal Flow

Our AV system consists of several interconnected components that work together to capture, process, and distribute audio-visual content.

### Primary Video Components

**Sony Camera**
- Captures the primary video of the service
- Provides high-quality SDI video output
- Professional-grade image quality for live streaming

**Blackmagic ATEM Production Studio 4K**
- Central video switcher and heart of our system
- Receives camera feed and graphics from AV1 PC
- Allows operator to switch between different video sources
- Outputs final "Program" feed to multiple destinations

**Blackmagic Smart Videohub**
- Video routing device that directs signals
- Sends Program feed to projector and Parents' Room
- Pre-configured for normal operation
- Manages signal distribution throughout the facility

### Graphics and Control

**AV1 PC (with EasyWorship)**
- Generates song lyrics and sermon slides
- Creates all on-screen graphics and announcements
- Output sent directly to ATEM switcher
- Primary computer for service presentation

**Elgato Stream Deck**
- Programmable keypad for one-touch operation
- Remote control for EasyWorship and ATEM switcher
- Streamlines common tasks during services
- Reduces complexity for operators

### Streaming and Audio

**Resi Decoder**
- Receives live video streams from other campuses
- Enables broadcasting of remote services
- Allows multi-campus connectivity
- Specialized streaming hardware

**Allen & Heath Audio Mixer**
- Handles all audio routing and mixing
- Receives audio from AV1 PC via ethernet
- Provides sound to main hall and parents' room
- Professional audio processing and distribution

## Signal Flow Diagram

```
Sony Camera → ATEM Switcher ← AV1 PC (Graphics)
     ↓              ↓
Video Hub → [Main Projector]
     ↓              ↓
[Parents' Room] [Online Stream]
```

## Output Destinations

Our system serves three primary destinations:

1. **Main Hall Projector** - Primary congregational display
2. **Parents' Room TV** - Separate viewing area for families
3. **Online Streaming** - Connection to other ARISE campuses

This integrated approach ensures consistent, high-quality audio-visual presentation across all viewing locations.
