### Sony PXW-Series Camera

<Callout type="warning" emoji="📷">
Handle camera equipment with care - professional broadcast equipment.
</Callout>

**Function:** Primary video capture device
- Professional-grade video quality
- SDI output for broadcast applications
- Manual and automatic exposure controls
- Designed for live event production

**Tripod Operation:**
To mount or unmount from the Vinten tripod:

1. **Locking Latch** - Small lever that secures camera plate
   - Prevents camera from sliding when locked
   - Always engage before releasing hands from camera

2. **Release Button** - Allows camera plate removal
   - Press to slide camera off tripod head
   - Ensure locking latch is disengaged first

**Safety Notes:**
- Always support camera weight when mounting/unmounting
- Check locking latch is secure before operating
- Use lens cap when not in use to protect optics
